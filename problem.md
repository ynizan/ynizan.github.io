A restaurant owner in Austin, Texas needs to navigate:
- Business license (city)
- Food handler permits (state)
- Health department inspection (county)
- Liquor license (state + city)
- Sign permits (city)
- Building permits (city)
- Fire safety inspection (city)
- Music license (federal - ASCAP/BMI)

**Result**: 6-12 months, $10,000+ in fees, endless paperwork

## Why This Hasn't Been Solved

1. **Fragmentation is extreme** - 90,000+ jurisdictions with unique systems
2. **Low digitization** - Many processes still paper-based or poorly digitized
3. **Data lock-in** - Government systems don't talk to each other
4. **Chicken-and-egg problem** - Hard to build without coverage, hard to get coverage without scale
5. **Traditional tech approach failed** - Tried to change government systems vs. abstract them away
